Analysis

This Saying is a facet of Epicurus' teaching on friendship and interpersonal relationships in general. We enjoy seeing those near us the most if we have had a positive experience with our first acquaintance. If we retain a pleasant recollection of our first meeting as agreeable, or if at least recall that it showed a mutual willingness to make it so, we are most likely to be glad to see them now and again.

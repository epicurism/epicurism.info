Pyrrho

From Epicurus Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Pyrrho [Πυρρων] (ca. 360 BCE - ca. 270 BCE), a Greek philosopher from Elis, was in Classical antiquity credited as being the first skeptic philosopher and inspiration for the school known as Pyrrhonism founded by Aenesidemus in the 1st century BC.
